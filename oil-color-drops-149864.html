Oil Color Drops
​
​These paintings are a visual representation of the beauty and complexity of the universe. The use of abstract oil color drops, streaks, stripes, angles, sun, moon, futuristic lines, and symbols creates a sense of movement and energy that is both dynamic and harmonious. The chiaroscuro technique adds depth and dimension to the paintings, creating a sense of space that invites the viewer to explore. Each painting is meant to be experienced on a personal level, with each viewer finding their own meaning in the shapes and colors. The sun and moon represent the duality of life, while the futuristic lines and symbols suggest a world beyond our own. The use of contrasting dark and light colors creates a sense of tension that is both exciting and calming.
We created paintings that challenge the viewer to think about the world in new ways. Abstract art allows each viewer to interpret it in their own way based on their own experiences and beliefs. This means that these paintings can be interpreted in many different ways depending on the viewer’s perspective.
In terms of terminology, abstract art is often described as non-representational art that does not depict objects or scenes from the real world. Instead, it focuses on the use of color, shape, line, and form to create a unique visual language that communicates emotions, ideas, and experiences. Different colors can be associated with different emotions. For example, red can represent passion or anger, while blue can represent calmness or sadness.
The painting is meant to be experienced on a personal level, with each viewer finding their own meaning in the shapes and colors. The sun and moon represent the duality of life, while the futuristic lines and symbols suggest a world beyond our own. The use of contrasting dark and light colors creates a sense of tension that is both exciting and calming.
Ultimately, the goal as an artist is to create a painting that inspires people to think about the world in new ways. We hope the work will encourage viewers to explore their own creativity and imagination.  -ALF
