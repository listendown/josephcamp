Watercolor Drops
​
​Water is the source of life, but also a powerful and unpredictable force of nature. I have always been fascinated by the fluidity and dynamism of water, and how it can create different shapes and patterns depending on its interaction with other elements. In my Watercolor drops series, I use the technique of dripping watercolors on paper to explore the possibilities and variations of this medium. I let the watercolors flow freely on the paper, creating abstract forms that evoke a sense of movement, energy, and spontaneity. I also experiment with different colors and contrasts to create visual harmony and balance.
​ My Watercolor drops series is an expression of my playful and creative spirit, as well as my appreciation for the beauty and complexity of nature. I enjoy the process of dripping watercolors on paper, as it allows me to be flexible and intuitive, as well as to embrace the unexpected and the accidental. I also like to challenge myself by working with different sizes and formats, from small sketches to large-scale paintings. My Watercolor drops series is a reflection of my artistic journey, as well as my contribution to the contemporary art scene.
                                                      +Joseph Camp
